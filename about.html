THE ARENA



What is it all about ?


This website is an arena where you compete with yourself,you compete with your previous performance. You improve the quality of story that you write with every picture you practise. You improve your core qualities by investing in self growth and development and by practising story writing here you improve the way you showcase your true qualities that you genuinely possess.Because showcasing your qualities is an important part of story writing in PP&DT's and TAT's.

This website dont offer any guidance or coaching to get recommended,you should invest in yourself and gain knowledge from other resources about the SSB procedure and come to this website to implement what your have learnt and improve the quality that you write.

Why this site came into existense ?


Recently i started learning web development and as a practise I wanted to build a project. I thought building a project that dont serve any purpose is of no use.So I thought a lot and came up with an idea that would serve atleast some purpose(this is what I think). And as a result 'The Arena' came into existense with a purpose of helping defence aspirants tp provide a platform to practise proper time bound story writing for PP&DT's and TAT's.

Its an attempt to help the community of defense aspirants.

With "LOVE" dedicated to the community of defense aspirants.
